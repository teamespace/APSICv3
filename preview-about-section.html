About the Award

The APSIC Environmental Hygiene Excellence Award is an invaluable recognition for healthcare facilities across Asia Pacific.

The AEHEA has been established as a regional platform to identify, recognise, honour, celebrate, and share best practices in environmental hygiene that demonstrate excellence in championing and sustaining the highest standard of hygiene and safety.

We would encourage nominees that have meaningfully contributed to this region.

Commitment to IPC

Facilities that demonstrate a sustained, organisation-wide commitment to infection prevention and control as a core pillar of patient care.

Innovative Practices

Programmes that introduce evidence-based innovation to improve environmental hygiene standards, protocols, and outcomes across the facility.

Quality of Care

Organisations where high environmental hygiene standards directly translate to measurable improvements in patient safety and clinical outcomes.
